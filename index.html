EMMA CREASEY

A jounrey through art. Using traditional storytelling methods such as scenery, character, narrative and tension, Emma creates imagined worlds that explore the human experience.

Emma Creasey creates intimate and curious paintings that imbue the mundane with a sense of opulence. Exploring the possibilities in visual story-telling, much of Emmas work focuses on the intimacy of sharing food. While each painting has a narrative of its own, Emmas works are linked through their tendency to leave the viewer feeling as though they have stumbled upon a private moment.

From abundant feasts abandoned in a forest clearing, to bright interiors that tread the boundary between comforting and somehow sinister, Emma's paintings dance between the weird and the familiar. By concocting a distinct mood through a mixture of food, homes and bodies, her scenes are charged with a narrative surrealism that's not often seen.
